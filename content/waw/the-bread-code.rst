Pull Dough
----------

+-----------------+------------+------------+
| Ingredients     | Baker's-%  | Weight (g) |
+=================+============+============+
| Flour (00, 500) | 100        | 450        |
+-----------------+------------+------------+
| Water           | 57         | 260        |
+-----------------+------------+------------+
| Salt            | 1.5        | 7          |
+-----------------+------------+------------+
| Oil             | 1.3        | 6          |
+-----------------+------------+------------+

Process
~~~~~~~

#. Mix sift flower with salt
#. Add slowly the water (room temperature) whilst mixing the dough.
#. And the oil.
#. Knead the dough for 20 minutes, ball it
#. Brush the dough with oil and let it rest +20 min

Pizza Dough
-----------


+-----------------+------------+---------------------+
| Ingredients     | Baker's-%  | Weight per ball (g) |
+=================+============+=====================+
| Flour (00, 500) | 100        | 295                 |
+-----------------+------------+---------------------+
| Water           | 63         | 186                 |
+-----------------+------------+---------------------+
| Salt            | 3          | 8,85                |
+-----------------+------------+---------------------+
| Oil             | 2          | 5,9                 |
+-----------------+------------+---------------------+
| Fresh Yeast     | 0,75       | 2,21                |
+-----------------+------------+---------------------+
| Sugar           | 1          | 2,95                |
+-----------------+------------+---------------------+
| Malt Flour*     | 2          | 5,9                 |
+-----------------+------------+---------------------+


Process
~~~~~~~

#. Mix Flour with malt flour.
#. Dissolve the fresh yeast and sugar in the water.
#. Add 10% of the flour to the water and mix.
#. Now add the salt and mix until it's almost dissolved.
#. Add slowly the rest of the flour whilst mixing the dough.
#. Knead the dough for 2 minutes and add the oil.
#. Knead for another 2 minutes.
#. Let the dough rest for 20 minutes, stretch and fold the dough and then ball it. Repeat this step two more times every 20 minutes. (The dough temperature never rose above 22°C/71°F)
#. Brush the dough with oil and let it rest in the fridge (~8°C/46°F) for 24 to 72 hours. (I let it rest for 48 hours)
#. Let the dough come to room temperature (2-3 hours).
#. Preheat the oven (at least 1 hour) with the baking stone/steel to around 270°C/520°F. (I used a stone and pizza screen. Might get a baking steel one day.)
#. Bake pizza with top and lower heat element for 7 minutes then turn on broiler for 1 to 2 minutes.
