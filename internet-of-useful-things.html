House On The Beach

Example of usage lorawan and five devices to measure temperature from all over the house.

Purpose of this dashboard is visualize air temperature measurements, to eliminate all the doubts of "is cold or warm", and if "not" there is no excuse to fire up the heating:)

Summary

And we are all happy now! Things looks to work as expected. The big issue is the lack of "external isolation" of the house, and consequences are - extreme temperature leaks!
